Student Executive Speech

by Howard Leoputra

Jun 2023

Good morning teachers, staff, and fellow students. How are you? My name is Howard. I am excited to be standing here as a candidate for student executive.

As a student, I believe that I can contribute ideas to make the school a better place of learning, growth, and friendship.

If elected, I will devote my time and work diligently to ensure that our voices are heard and our needs are met.

My goal is to make sure that all students feel valued and it is also important to give back to our community.

I am devoted to collaborate with students and teachers to create meaningful changes to enhance our school experience.

Thank you for considering me for this important role and again, I am looking forward to the opportunity to serve you all.

Thank you
